## Who Is Kuat Systems Engineering?

KSE is a 6,200-person manufacturer of precision starship components —
navigation arrays, shield generators, hyperdrive couplings — supplying
both New Republic defense contractors and civilian freight operators.
Headquartered on Kuat, with major operations in Corellia and Mandalore.

They have been our customer since 2023, currently on the VANGUARD
Enterprise Intelligence Platform at **480,000 credits ARR**.
Contract term: August 31, 2023 → August 31, 2026.
Renewal call scheduled for late June 2026.

### Why They Bought Us

KSE's previous vendor, Nexus Data Works, suffered a catastrophic breach
in 2022. Imperial remnant operatives extracted 14 months of hyperspace
routing data — effectively a live map of New Republic supply chain
movements. Three KSE clients filed breach-of-contract claims.

Lyra Antilles (then Head of Infrastructure, now VP Technology) ran the
vendor replacement process herself. She put 11 vendors through a 53-point
security questionnaire. We were the only one who passed clean.

She has been our internal champion ever since. She quotes our response
times at KSE all-hands. She referred us to Corellian Engineering Group
(closed, 140,000 credits ARR). She is the reason this account exists.

### What VANGUARD Does For Them

KSE uses VANGUARD to optimize hyperspace lane routing for their internal
logistics network — 400+ component transfers per week across six systems.
The platform's predictive re-routing module saved them 2.1 million credits
in fuel expenditure in FY2025. That number goes on slide 2 of every
renewal deck.
